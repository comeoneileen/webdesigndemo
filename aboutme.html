About Me

I call Minnesota home, but I'm originally from Illinois. I was born in Chicago and lived there until I was 15. When I was in 10th grade, I left home to go to boarding school in rural Illinois. After graduating from high school, I moved to Minnesota to go to St. Olaf College. Now I've lived here in the frozen North for ten years, and I think of it as my home!

I am definitely a person who likes to keep busy! Monday through Friday, I work at Humboldt High School in the AOIT program. In the afternoons, I teach other classes: Monday/Wednesday/Friday, I teach theater; Tuesday and Thursday, I teach a class about making video games. On the weekends, I also work at a donut shop. I don't always have as much time for sleep or hobbies as I'd like, but I'm okay with that for now.
